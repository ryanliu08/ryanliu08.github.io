徽记 / Shield
"...此外,又拿着信德当作藤牌,可以灭尽那恶者一切的火箭" (Ephesians 6:16 以弗所书) 

符号的意义: 盾牌代表了我们的信心。十字架代表了耶稣基督的牺牲。白鸽代表了上帝的圣灵。圣经代表了上帝的话语。外框代表了合一和团结。 

颜色的意义: 蓝色代表了活水。红色代表耶稣的宝血。白色代表了光和圣洁。黄色代表了尊贵和荣耀。

"... take up the shield of faith, with which you can extinguish all the flaming arrows of the evil one." 

Meanings of Symbols: The shield represents our faith. The cross represents the sacrifice of Jesus. The dove represents the Spirit of God. The Bible is the Word of God. The frame represents oneness or unity. 

Meanings of Colors: The blue color represents the living water. The red color represents the blood. The white color represents light and purity. The gold color represents royalty and glory.
