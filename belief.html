信仰宣言  / Statement of Faith


使徒信经
​
我信上帝，
即全能之圣父，
创造天地之主。

我信其独生之圣子，我主耶稣基督；
我信耶稣为受圣灵感动之童贞女马利亚所生；
我信耶稣在本丢彼拉多手下遇难，被钉十字架，死而埋葬；
我信其降至阴间，第三日从死复活；
我信其升天，坐于全能之天主父之右；
我信其日后必自彼处降临，审判生人死人。

我信圣灵；
我信圣而公之教会, 
我信圣相通功；
我信赦罪之恩；
我信身死后必复活；
我信永生。

阿们。
​
The Apostles Creed

I believe in God, 
the Father almighty, 
creator of heaven and earth.

I believe in Jesus Christ, God's only Son, our Lord, 
who was conceived by the Holy Spirit, 
born of the Virgin Mary, 
suffered under Pontius Pilate, 
was crucified, died, and was buried; 
he descended to the dead. 
On the third day he rose again; 
he ascended into heaven, 
he is seated at the right hand of the Father, 
and he will come again to judge the living and the dead.

I believe in the Holy Spirit, 
the holy catholic church, 
the communion of saints, 
the forgiveness of sins, 
the resurrection of the body, 
and the life everlasting.

AMEN.
Notes:
这是最早的基督教信经, 很多人相信它出现于二世纪中页。
1. This oldest known Christian creed was believed to be drawn up as early as the middle of the second century. 
2. The term "catholic church" refers to the universal church of Jesus Christ.
